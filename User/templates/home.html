About Us

The Academy of Technology or AOT is an undergraduate engineering college in West Bengal, India. It was established in 2003 by Ananda Educational Development and Charitable Organisation, a trust. The college is affiliated with Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology and all the programmes are approved by the All India Council for Technical Education.
